A little about Protected Areas

These are the areas that are protected or conserved due to its ecological or cultural values 🌈.Such areas doesnt have accesiblity of modifications for any purposes.It is mainly for long-term conservation of nature 🌱.Almost every country of the world 🌏have many conservation areas as it restores the heritage of the particular area. Marae Moana is the largest protected area in the world with a total area larger than Mexico 💫.

Categories of Conversation Areas 🍃:

  1. Category Ia – Strict nature reserve
  2. Category Ib – Wilderness Area
  3. Category II – National park
  4. III – Natural monument or Feature
  5. Category IV – Habitat/Species Management Area
  6. Category V – Protected Landscape/Seascape
  7. Category VI – Protected Area with sustainable use of natural resources
